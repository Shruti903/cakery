About Us

Founded in 2015, RSCakery Bliss is a boutique bakery dedicated to creating handcrafted cakes, pastries, and desserts made with love and care. Our mission is to bring joy to every occasion through our delicious creations.

Our Philosophy

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Each item is made from scratch daily, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

Our Team

Led by award-winning pastry chef Shruti Raj, our team of talented bakers and decorators bring creativity and passion to every creation. With over 15 years of combined experience, we're committed to excellence in both taste and presentation.
